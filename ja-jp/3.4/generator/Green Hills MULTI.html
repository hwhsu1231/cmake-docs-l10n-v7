Green Hills MULTI¶

Generates Green Hills MULTI project files (experimental, work-in-progress).

Customizations are available through the following cache variables:

  • GHS_BSP_NAME
  • GHS_CUSTOMIZATION
  • GHS_GPJ_MACROS
  • GHS_OS_DIR

注釈

This generator is deemed experimental as of CMake 3.4.3 and is still a work in progress. Future versions of CMake may make breaking changes as the generator matures.
